Vincent Willem van Gogh

1853.3.30 ~ 1890.7.29

A Dutch painter, generally regarded as one of the greatest painters in Western art history. He created all of his works (900 drawings and 1100 studies) in just 10 years before committing suicide due to mental illness.

The Starry Night

Dutch painter Vincent van Gogh's most well-known work, and was painted by Van Gogh while he was suffering from a mental illness. In 1889, in a mental hospital in Saint-Remy, Van Gogh recalled the pain caused by a mental illness and described it as a whirlpool in a painting.

SunFlowers

This is a still life by Vincent van Gogh. There are two versions of this painting: the first is a sunflower on the floor painted in Paris in 1887, and the second is a sunflower in a vase painted in Arles a year later.

Wheat Field with Crows

It is erroneously known as Van Gogh's last painting. It is known that he painted this painting and committed suicide on the spot. However, there is no evidence to support that idea. Van Gogh's Chronicle, created by Jan Hulsker, states that Van Gogh painted seven more paintings after this painting.
